The President and the Members of the IISc Alumni Association are pleased to announce SUROLIA AWARD instituted by prof. Avadesha Surolia, SERB, Distinguished Fellow, Honorary Professor, Molecular Bio-Physics Unit, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. The award is flagship initiative of Prof. Avadesha Surolia and is hosted by IISc Alumni Association, Bengaluru

Indian Institute of Science Alumni Association invites nomination for the Fourth Dr. Srinivasan Rajagopalan Award for the year 2023, instituted by the family of Dr. Rajagopalan. Dr. S.Rajagopalan was a Research Scholar (1938-41) and then aFellow(1952) of IISc. The family c​contributed a lumpsum amount to IIScAA and requested to institute this award once in two years to a young applied scientist. The biennial award for 2023 to a scientist carries a cash prize (Apprx: Rupees One Lakh) and a Citation. The award function is tentatively scheduled to be held in IISc, Bangalore, during April 2023 and the exact date will beintimated to the awardee well in advance. The awardee is required to receive the award in person and deliver a technical talk during the award function highlighting his/her significant contributions.
