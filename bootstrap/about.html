About Us

Welcome to the Indian Institute of Science Alumni Association - a registered body of the Alumni members of the Indian Institute of Science. Nestled in the lush greenery of the Institute campus at Bengaluru, the Indian Institute of Science Alumni Association (IIScAA), is the organisation of the Alumni of the country’s #1 University - The Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, whose sole vision and mission is to create a network of its Alumni spread across the globe under single platform and to engage together to promote the ideals of its' Alma Mater and to give back to its Alma Mater. The Indian Institute of Science Alumni Association (IIScAA) was founded on 20th September 1975, by its distinguished Founder President Dr. S.R. Valluri, Director, National Aerospace Laboratories (NAL), Bengaluru, with the chief architect of the Indian Space Programme (Now ISRO), Padma Vibhushan Prof. Sathish Dhawan, Director, Indian Institute of Science (1962-1981) as its first Patron. The Alumni Association is a body duly registered and incorporated under the Karnataka Societies Act of 1960; governed and managed by its Bye Laws and Procedures. With its illustrious journey of 111 years in the field of education, the Institute has record of producing nearly 25000+ Scientist and Engineers in various fields. The members of this Association are privileged to be part of this group.
